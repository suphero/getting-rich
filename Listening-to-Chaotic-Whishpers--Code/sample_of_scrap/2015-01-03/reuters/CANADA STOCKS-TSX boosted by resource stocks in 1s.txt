CANADA STOCKS-TSX boosted by resource stocks in 1st session of new year TORONTO, Jan 2 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index closed higher on Friday, led by energy and materials stocks in the first session of 2015. The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index  unofficially closed up 121.21 points, or 0.83 percent, at 14,753.65.
