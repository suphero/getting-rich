Yahoo search engine down shortly after Bing outage SAN FRANCISCO (Reuters) - Yahoo Inc’s search engine was down on Friday, shortly after Microsoft Corp’s search engine, Bing, was also briefly unavailable before recovering. Users who typed search.yahoo.com got an error message saying that Yahoo engineers were working to resolve the issue. Bing powers Yahoo search under a 10-year deal announced in 2009. “This morning, some of our customers experienced a brief, isolated services interruption which has now been resolved,” Microsoft said.
