CANADA STOCKS-TSX rises at open, lifted by financial sector TORONTO, Jan 2 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index opened higher on Friday, led by financial stocks in the first trading session of 2015. The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index  was up 43.50 points, or 0.30 percent, at 14,675.94 shortly after the open.
