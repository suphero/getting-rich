CORRECTED-Medical issue forces Qatar Airways flight to make unscheduled UK landing LONDON, Jan 1 (Reuters) - A Qatar Airways flight from New York will make an unscheduled landing at Britain’s Manchester Airport due to a medical issue on board, the airport said on Thursday. “We appear to have a minor medical issue on board so we’re diverting the flight to Manchester as a precaution before it carries on to its destination in Doha,” an airport spokeswoman said.
