A Donut-of-the-Month Calendar, Berry Jams and a Teacup for Loose-Leaf Pours Here’s a fresh take on the old cheesecake pinup. In her donut-of-the-month calendar, San Francisco artist April V. Walters offers a taste of the Bay Area’s finest fried rounds. For the second year running, Ms. Walters has depicted her favorites in watercolor, usually painting the pastries in the shops themselves. The year begins with a peanut-studded chocolate-cake variety from the beloved All Stars Donuts.
