Florida Judge Clears Way for Gay Marriage A federal judge cleared the way for same-sex couples in Florida to begin marrying Tuesday, a notable victory for gay-marriage supporters in the nation’s third-most-populous state.In a ruling Thursday, U.S. District Judge Robert Hinkle in Tallahassee clarified the scope of his August order finding the state’s same-sex marriage ban unconstitutional. At the time, he issued a preliminary injunction, but stayed his order until Jan. 5.
