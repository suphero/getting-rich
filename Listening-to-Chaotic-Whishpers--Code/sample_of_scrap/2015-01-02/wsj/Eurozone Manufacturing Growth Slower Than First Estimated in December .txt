Eurozone Manufacturing Growth Slower Than First Estimated in December Activity in the eurozone’s manufacturing sector grew at a slower pace than first estimated in December, maintaining the pressure on the European Central Bank to step up its stimulus campaign. The headline measure from data firm Markit’s monthly survey of purchasing managers of around 3,000 manufacturers rose to 50.6 from 50.1 in November, but was lower than the preliminary estimate of 50.8.
