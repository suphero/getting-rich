At Amazon, $7.4 Billion Paper Loss for Bezos The Amazon holdings of Mr. Bezos, chief executive, lost about $7.4 billion in value over the course of 2014 as the e-commerce giant’s shares fell more than 22%. Still, Mr. Bezos’s 84 million shares, equal to 18.3% of the company, rang in the New Year with a value of roughly $26.1 billion.
