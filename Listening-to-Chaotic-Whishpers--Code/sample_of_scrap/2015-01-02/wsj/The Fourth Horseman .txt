The Fourth Horseman  Of the four most significant European dictators of the 20th century—Hitler, Stalin, Mussolini and Franco—the last survived the longest and was the most successful, as Stanley G. Payne and Jesús Palacios suggest in their perceptive biography. Hitler and Mussolini perished in absolute defeat.
