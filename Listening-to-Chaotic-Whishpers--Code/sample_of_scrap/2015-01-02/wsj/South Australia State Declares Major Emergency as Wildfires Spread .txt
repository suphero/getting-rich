South Australia State Declares Major Emergency as Wildfires Spread
		
			
		
	 SYDNEY—South Australia’s state police commissioner declared a major emergency as wildfires burned out of control in two separate areas near the city of Adelaide.The Country Fire Service said more than 100 firefighters were tackling fires burning in the southern Mount Lofty Ranges, northeast of the city, which it said was spreading in “a very erratic manner.” It said a separate fire southeast of the city also continued to grow.
