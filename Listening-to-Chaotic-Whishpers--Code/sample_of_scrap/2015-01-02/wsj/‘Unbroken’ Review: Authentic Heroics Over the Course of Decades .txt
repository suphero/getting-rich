‘Unbroken’ Review: Authentic Heroics Over the Course of Decades A tale of endurance, “Unbroken” takes endurance to sit through. That may have been inevitable, to some degree, given the tale’s harrowing nature and its inherent lack of suspense; the title isn’t “Broken,” so there’s not much doubt of the outcome. But it’s certainly regrettable, because this long and increasingly sluggish film version of the Laura Hillenbrand book celebrates an American life of singular heroism.
