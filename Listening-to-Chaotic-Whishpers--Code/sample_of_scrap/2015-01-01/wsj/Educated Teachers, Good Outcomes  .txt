Educated Teachers, Good Outcomes  Despite Katharine B. Stevens’s argument that “young children don’t need a qualified workforce” (“Here Come the Child-Care Cops,” op-ed, Dec. 21), the research clearly shows that child care and preschool teachers with a formal education from an accredited college or university provide better quality care and produce better outcomes in their students.
