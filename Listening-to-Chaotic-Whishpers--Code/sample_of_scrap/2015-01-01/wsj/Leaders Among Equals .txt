Leaders Among Equals Coming more than 10 years after the accession of Poland and seven other Central and Eastern European states into the European Union, Donald Tusk’s elevation to president of the European Council is not just symbolically important. It reflects a real transformation that has taken place over the course of the past decade. The countries that joined the EU in 2004 and 2007 have grown in their material wealth, but also in their self-confidence and expectations of themselves. The political and economic changes are such that it no longer makes sense to talk of the “new accession states” as if they form a separate category....
