Five Wins by New Year’s Day? Time to Play the NBA Lottery When the Knicks take the court Friday against the Detroit Pistons, they’ll be in the odd position of envying their opponents’ eight total wins, even as the NBA season approaches its halfway point. The question, though, is whether the 5-29 Knicks should even want to boost their paltry win total at this point. Righting the ship could be easier if they lose enough to land an impact player in the 2015 NBA draft.
