BRIEF-Merlin Properties completes financing of its unit * Said on Tuesday it completed the financing of its unit Tree Inversiones Inmobiliaria SOCIMI SA (Tree) via a loan of 940 million euros ($1.14 billion) * The syndicated loan, with a maturity of 10 years, is secured by the asset portfolio of Tree, rented on a long-term basis to BBVA * The company will allocate part of the proceeds to repay existing debt of Tree due 2017, which currently amounts to 828 million euros ($1 billion) and to pay the costs of the operation * The financing banks are CaixaBank, Santander, BNP Paribas, Credit Agricole, Banco Popular and Societe Generale, among others All quotes delayed a minimum of 15 minutes.
