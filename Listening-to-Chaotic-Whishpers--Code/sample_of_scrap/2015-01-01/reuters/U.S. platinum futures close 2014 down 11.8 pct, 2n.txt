U.S. platinum futures close 2014 down 11.8 pct, 2nd year lower NEW YORK, Dec 31 (Reuters) - The spot U.S. January platinum futures contract settled down 0.8 percent at $1,208.90 per ounce on Wednesday, and closed 2014 down 11.8 percent versus the end of 2013. This is the second straight year lower for the most-active contract.   (Reporting by Marcy Nicholson; Editing by Chris Reese)All quotes delayed a minimum of 15 minutes.
