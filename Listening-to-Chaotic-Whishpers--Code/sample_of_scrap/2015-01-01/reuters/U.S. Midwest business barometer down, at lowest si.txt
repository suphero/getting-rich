U.S. Midwest business barometer down, at lowest since July Dec 31 (Reuters) - The pace of business activity in the U.S. Midwest slowed in December, a report showed on Wednesday. The Institute for Supply Management-Chicago Business Barometer declined to 58.3, its lowest reading since July, after a November reading of 60.8. A reading above 50 indicates expansion in the regional economy.    (Reporting by David Gaffen; Editing by Meredith Mazzilli)All quotes delayed a minimum of 15 minutes.
