Russia's Gazprom says Ukraine has paid $150 mln for Jan gas MOSCOW, Dec 31 (Reuters) - Russia’s top natural gas producer Gazprom said on Wednesday that Ukrainian energy firm Naftogaz had paid it $150 million for gas supplies in January. Last week, Gazprom said it had received a $1.65 billion payment from Ukraine to cover the country’s gas debts.
