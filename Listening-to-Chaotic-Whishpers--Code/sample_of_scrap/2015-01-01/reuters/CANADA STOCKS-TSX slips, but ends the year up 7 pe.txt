CANADA STOCKS-TSX slips, but ends the year up 7 percent TORONTO, Dec 31 (Reuters) - Canada’s main stock index closed little changed on Wednesday, nudged slightly lower by financial shares, but it ended the year up 7 percent. The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX composite index  unofficially closed down 7.6 points, or 0.05 percent, at 14,632.44.
