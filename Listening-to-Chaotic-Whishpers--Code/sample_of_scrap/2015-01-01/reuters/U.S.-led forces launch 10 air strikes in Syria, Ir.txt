U.S.-led forces launch 10 air strikes in Syria, Iraq WASHINGTON (Reuters) - The U.S.-led coalition launched seven air strikes in Syria and three in Iraq on Wednesday against Islamic State militants, the Combined Joint Task Force said in a statement. In Syria, five of the air strikes were near Kobani and two were near al Hasakah, the task force said in a statement.
