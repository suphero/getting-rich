China to raise export rebates for corn starch, textiles BEIJING, Dec 31 (Reuters) - China will raise export rebates on Thursday for corn starch to 13 percent and that for some textile products to 17 percent, the Ministry of Finance said, a move to help loss-making farmers and textile makers. The rebate increase for corn starch will be valid until end of 2015, the government said on Wednesday.
