Indonesia says one body from AirAsia plane was wearing life jacket JAKARTA (Reuters) - A body recovered from the crashed AirAsia plane on Wednesday was wearing a life jacket, an official with Indonesia’s search and rescue agency said. “This morning, we recovered a total of four bodies and one of them was wearing a life jacket,” said Tatang Zaenudin, an official with the search and rescue agency.
